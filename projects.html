Graduation Project: A Multimodal Deep Learning Approach for Fake News Detection Across Text and Visual Modalities

2026

Designing a deep learning system to detect fake news by analyzing both text and images, and focusing on a lightweight and efficient architecture.

Approach & Methods: Using TinyBERT as the text encoder and MobileNetV3 as the image encoder, implementing projection heads to map embeddings to the same dimensional space, and exploring feature-level fusion strategies.

Marwed: A Tourist Guide App for Saudi Arabia

2025

Marwed is a mobile application that centralizes tourism information in Saudi Arabia, providing real-time updates on events, attractions, and services, with multilingual support and seamless booking features.

Key Features: User accounts, search/filter attractions, event booking, ratings & reviews, multilingual support.
